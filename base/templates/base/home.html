Premier League Sentimeter

About

This site uses twitter data to gauge the overall sentiment about a soccer team and its players.

Team Score

For each team, its overall sentiment score for the past month is displayed in a line graph. This score is determined by averaging the sentiment score of a sample of tweets over a given time period.

Tweet Locations

Locations of tweets about a particular team are displayed via twitter geolocation data. Note that this data only exists for a small fraction of tweets.

Player Scores

Player scores are determined the same way as those of teams. means a positive change, means a negative change, and means the score was unchanged or no tweets were found. Note that small sample size may skew some player scores.

Navigation

The sidebar menu links to each team. The compare tool allows for the data of two teams to be displayed simultaneously and compared.
